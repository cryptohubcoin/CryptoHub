What is Solana (SOL)?

Solana (SOL) is a high-performance blockchain capable of processing thousands of transactions per second at very low cost. Launched in 2020, it uses a unique proof-of-history mechanism combined with proof-of-stake. Solana hosts a thriving ecosystem of DeFi protocols, NFT marketplaces, and Web3 applications.
