What is Chainlink (LINK)?

Chainlink (LINK) is a decentralized oracle network that connects smart contracts to real-world data, APIs, and payment systems. It plays a critical role in DeFi by providing reliable price feeds and external information to blockchains. LINK is the token used to pay node operators for their data services.
