What is the Fear & Greed Index? +

The Fear & Greed Index is a market sentiment indicator that measures the overall emotional state of the crypto market on a scale from 0 (Extreme Fear) to 100 (Extreme Greed). It is calculated using factors including price volatility, market momentum, social media sentiment, and market dominance. Extreme fear can signal buying opportunities, while extreme greed may indicate an overheated market.

What is the Altcoin Season Index? +

The Altcoin Season Index measures whether the market is in "Bitcoin Season" or "Altcoin Season." When altcoins are outperforming Bitcoin over a 90-day period, it is considered Altcoin Season. A score above 75 indicates Altcoin Season; below 25 indicates Bitcoin Season. This helps traders understand where capital is flowing in the market.

Bitcoin Ecosystem (BRC-20 & Runes)
What are BRC-20 tokens? +

BRC-20 is an experimental token standard built on the Bitcoin blockchain using the Ordinals protocol. Unlike ERC-20 tokens on Ethereum, BRC-20 tokens are inscribed directly onto satoshis (the smallest unit of Bitcoin). Popular BRC-20 tokens include ORDI, SATS, RATS, PIZZA, and CATS. They can be traded on marketplaces like Magic Eden and UniSat.

What are Runes? +

Runes is a newer fungible token protocol on Bitcoin introduced by Casey Rodarmor (the creator of Ordinals) in April 2024. Runes are more efficient than BRC-20 tokens and use Bitcoin's UTXO model natively. Popular Runes include DOG•GO•TO•THE•MOON, RSIC•GENESIS•RUNE, and COOK. They are traded on Magic Eden's Runes marketplace.
