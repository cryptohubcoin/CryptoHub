What is Ethereum (ETH)?

Ethereum (ETH) is the leading smart contract platform, launched in 2015 by Vitalik Buterin. It introduced programmable, self-executing contracts that power decentralized applications, DeFi, and NFTs. After transitioning to proof-of-stake in 2022, Ethereum became far more energy-efficient while remaining the backbone of Web3.
