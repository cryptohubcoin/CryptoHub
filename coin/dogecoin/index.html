What is Dogecoin (DOGE)?

Dogecoin (DOGE) began in 2013 as a meme coin featuring the Shiba Inu dog. Despite humorous origins, it grew into one of the most recognized cryptocurrencies, with a strong community and frequent celebrity endorsements. It is used for tipping and payments.
